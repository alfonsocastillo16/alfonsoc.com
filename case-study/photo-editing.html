Client: Self-Initiated Project
Project: Photo Editing

This self-initiated project was started with the goal of showcasing my photo editing capabilities. I chose to use this picture because of its several opportunities for enhancement. The end result is a picture that is more vibrant and features a model that looks many

years younger. This project was done entirely in Photoshop. The following adjustments were made: quality and lighting were improved, flyaway hair was removed, arm hair was removed, several areas were reshaped, wrinkles and creases were removed or lightened, veins were removed, and freckles were removed.
