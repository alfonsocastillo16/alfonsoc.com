Client: KIDSPORTS Invitational
Project: Branding, Logo Design, Graphic Design, Web Design, Web Development

The KIDSPORTS Invitational is an annual non-profit youth basketball tournament that provides a unique opportunity for kids to play at Matthew Knight Arena, home of the University of Oregon Ducks basketball teams. I was appointed as Creative Director for the 2014 KIDSPORTS Invitational with the goal of completely rebranding the event by developing a new identity. This new identity needed to reposition the KIDSPORTS Invitational brand while still remaining true to the event's values and message. The objectives for this project were to get teams registered, raise public awareness, create a sponsorable inventory of content, raise sales, develop new participants, and be a vehicle for marketing. I began by redesigning the KIDSPORTS Invitational logo and website

as both lacked professional brand design and gave off a poor first impression. The new logo and website designs both retained elements of their predecessor while giving off a modern and professional look with the intention of attracting new participants and sponsors. The website was then developed using HTML and CSS on Adobe Dreamweaver. New graphics were designed for the KIDSPORTS Invitational social media profile that were consistent with the new identity. Every series and campaign leading up to the event featured new graphics designed for them including a day of event campaign that utilized real-time graphics to drive audience engagement.
