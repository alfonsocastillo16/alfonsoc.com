Client: The Cauldron
Project: Branding, Logo Design

The Cauldron is a www.medium.com collection of sports stories, shared by a diverse and talented group of contributors with experience across the entire sports landscape. The Cauldron asked for a logo that met the objectives of being simple, memorable, and versatile. A logo that communicated its representation of an intense place about sports while remaining recognizable, legible, and not overwrought. A logo that would resonate with its audience and popped off the page but that also worked well on all forms of social media.

This logo is specifically designed to fit the needs of The Cauldron. It is a simple design that communicates the intensity and sportive nature of The Cauldron. The illustration pays homage to the clip art cauldron in an effort to advance its image without risking potential harm to brand awareness. This illustration features symbols from major sports to insure the logo is unmistakable and memorable. Very intense colors (Fire Red, Silver, Safety Yellow, and Onyx 2) were chosen to make the logo truly pop off the page, be very legible, and recognizable. Lastly, the logo is completely versatile and can be adapted for any need.
