Client: RTH Photography
Project: Branding, Logo Design, Graphic Design

RTH Photography is the alias of Ryan Hsu, an up-and-coming freelance photographer. I was asked to design a logo using the letters RTH with the objective of being able to be usable across serveral platforms. I approached the design by creating concepts in which the letters RTH would coincide in a way that would help describe the brand and be recognizable. The design concept that prevailed, inspired by the Canon PowerShot SX50 HS, used the letters to create the shape of a camera. The tail of the letter R is rounded to resemble the lens and the letters TH are slanted slightly to resemble the grip. The area above the letters is then closed off and contorted to resemble the flash to further give

shape to the logo. To finish off the design the gap left behind by where the shape of the flash is placed is then branded, as it is on most cameras, with the letters RTH in the brand typeface and the word photography is placed below the camera. I decided against adding too much effect to the logo and instead went with a flat design as visual simplicity is currently trending amongst brands. The main design features a subtle gradient that uses 3 different shades of red. Along with those 3 shades of red the brand also uses a light shade of gray, mostly for background purposes. Along with the logo, other branded material was also designed such as a business card, landing page/background, envelope, letter, and more.
